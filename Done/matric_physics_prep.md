## Chapter-wise Important Short Questions + Answers

### Chapter 10: Simple Harmonic Motion and Waves

**Q1: Simple Harmonic Motion (SHM) kya hai?**
A: Woh motion jismein restoring force displacement ke directly proportional ho aur direction opposite ho. Example: pendulum ka swing.

**Q2: Time Period kya hota hai?**
A: Ek complete vibration mein laga hua waqt. Unit: second (s). Formula: T = 1/f

**Q3: Frequency ki definition do.**
A: Ek second mein hone wali vibrations ki total number. Unit: Hertz (Hz). Formula: f = 1/T

**Q4: Wave motion ki types batao.**
A: (i) Transverse waves — particles wave ke direction ke perpendicular move karte hain (ii) Longitudinal waves — particles wave ke direction mein move karte hain

**Q5: Wavelength kya hai?**
A: Do consecutive crests ya troughs ke beech ki distance. Symbol: λ (lambda). Unit: meter

### Chapter 11: Sound

**Q1: Sound kaise produce hoti hai?**
A: Sound vibrating objects se produce hoti hai. Jab koi object vibrate karta hai to surrounding air molecules compress aur rarefy hote hain.

**Q2: Sound ka speed kis mein sabse zyada hota hai?**
A: Solids mein sabse zyada (5000 m/s steel mein), phir liquids (1500 m/s water), phir gases mein sabse kam (340 m/s air mein at 20°C).

**Q3: Echo kya hai?**
A: Jab sound kisi surface se reflect ho ke wapas aaye to usse echo kehte hain. Echo sunne ke liye minimum distance 17 meters honi chahiye.

**Q4: Loudness aur pitch mein farq batao.**
A: Loudness amplitude pe depend karti hai (zyada amplitude = zyada loud). Pitch frequency pe depend karti hai (zyada frequency = zyada sharp/high pitch).

**Q5: Ultrasound kya hai? Uses batao.**
A: 20,000 Hz se zyada frequency wali sound. Uses: medical scanning (ultrasound), cleaning, SONAR (samundar ki depth naapna).

### Chapter 12: Geometrical Optics (Light)

**Q1: Reflection ke laws batao.**
A: (i) Angle of incidence = Angle of reflection (∠i = ∠r) (ii) Incident ray, reflected ray, aur normal sab ek plane mein hote hain.

**Q2: Refraction kya hai?**
A: Jab light ek medium se doosre medium mein jaaye aur apna path change kare to refraction kehte hain. Example: pool ka paani shallow dikhna.

**Q3: Concave aur Convex mirror mein farq batao.**
A:
| Property | Concave Mirror | Convex Mirror |
|----------|---------------|---------------|
| Surface | Andar ki taraf curved | Bahar ki taraf curved |
| Image | Real + virtual dono | Sirf virtual |
| Use | Shaving mirror, torch | Side mirrors (vehicles) |

**Q4: Snell's Law likho.**
A: n₁ sin θ₁ = n₂ sin θ₂ (jahan n = refractive index, θ = angle)

**Q5: Total Internal Reflection kya hai?**
A: Jab light denser medium se rarer medium mein jaaye aur angle of incidence critical angle se zyada ho, to light reflect ho ke wapas aa jaati hai. Use: optical fiber, diamonds ki chamak.

### Chapter 13: Electrostatics

**Q1: Coulomb's Law likho.**
A: F = kq₁q₂/r² (jahan k = 9 × 10⁹ Nm²/C², q = charge, r = distance)

**Q2: Electric field kya hai?**
A: Woh space jahan kisi charge pe electric force act kare. Formula: E = F/q. Unit: N/C

**Q3: Capacitor kya karta hai?**
A: Capacitor charge store karta hai. Capacitance: C = Q/V. Unit: Farad (F).

**Q4: Electrostatic induction kya hai?**
A: Bina touch kiye kisi object mein charge produce karna — charged object paas laao to opposite charge attract hota hai.

---

### Chapter 14: Current Electricity

**Q1: Ohm's Law likho.**
A: V = IR (Voltage = Current × Resistance). Current voltage ke proportional hai jab resistance constant ho.

**Q2: Series aur Parallel circuit mein farq batao.**
A:
| Property | Series | Parallel |
|----------|--------|----------|
| Current | Same through all | Divides |
| Voltage | Divides | Same across all |
| Resistance | R_total = R₁+R₂+R₃ | 1/R_total = 1/R₁+1/R₂+1/R₃ |

**Q3: Kilowatt-hour (kWh) kya hai?**
A: Electrical energy ka unit. 1 kWh = 3.6 × 10⁶ Joules. Yeh bijli ka bill isi mein aata hai.

**Q4: Electric power ka formula likho.**
A: P = VI = I²R = V²/R. Unit: Watt (W).

---

### Chapter 15: Electromagnetism

**Q1: Electromagnetic induction kya hai?**
A: Changing magnetic field se EMF (voltage) produce hona. Faraday ne discover kiya.

**Q2: Transformer kya karta hai?**
A: AC voltage ko increase (step-up) ya decrease (step-down) karta hai. Formula: V₁/V₂ = N₁/N₂

**Q3: DC motor ka principle batao.**
A: Current-carrying conductor jab magnetic field mein ho to force experience karta hai (Fleming's Left Hand Rule).

**Q4: Lenz's Law kya hai?**
A: Induced current ki direction aisi hoti hai ke woh us change ka oppose kare jisne usse produce kiya.

## Important Numerical Problems (Solved)

### Numerical 1: Wave Speed
**Q:** Ek wave ki frequency 50 Hz hai aur wavelength 2m hai. Speed find karo.
**Solution:**
v = fλ
v = 50 × 2
**v = 100 m/s**

### Numerical 2: Ohm's Law
**Q:** Ek circuit mein voltage 12V hai aur resistance 4Ω hai. Current find karo.
**Solution:**
V = IR
I = V/R = 12/4
**I = 3 Amperes**

### Numerical 3: Electrical Energy
**Q:** 100W ka bulb 10 hours chale to kitni energy use hogi (kWh)?
**Solution:**
E = P × t
E = 100W × 10h = 1000 Wh
**E = 1 kWh**

### Numerical 4: Coulomb's Law
**Q:** 2μC aur 3μC ke do charges 0.3m ki distance pe hain. Force find karo.
**Solution:**
F = kq₁q₂/r²
F = (9×10⁹)(2×10⁻⁶)(3×10⁻⁶) / (0.3)²
F = (9×10⁹)(6×10⁻¹²) / 0.09
F = 54×10⁻³ / 0.09
**F = 0.6 N**

### Numerical 5: Transformer
**Q:** Primary coil mein 200 turns hain, secondary mein 1000. Input voltage 50V hai. Output voltage?
**Solution:**
V₁/V₂ = N₁/N₂
50/V₂ = 200/1000
V₂ = 50 × 1000/200
**V₂ = 250V (Step-up)**

### Numerical 6: Parallel Resistance
**Q:** 6Ω aur 3Ω parallel mein hain. Total resistance?
**Solution:**
1/R = 1/R₁ + 1/R₂
1/R = 1/6 + 1/3 = 1/6 + 2/6 = 3/6
**R = 2Ω**
